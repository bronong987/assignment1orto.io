Welcome to ORTO

Description

Welcome to ORTO, formerly called Bottle Tree Park, ORTO has been revamped and refurbished, yet retained its old kampong charms and rustic feel! The cool, scenic and relaxing ambience makes the park a fitting venue not only for family outings, but also for company events and parties. Accessible and conveniently located near Khatib MRT, ORTO offers a quick and easy respite from the city’s hustle and bustle, providing fun and recreational activities that are meant to help you relax, bond with family and friends, and get closer to nature. Scroll on down to see the outdoor activities we provide over here at ORTO! Guaranteed fun joy and laughter with your friends, family and your significant other!

Popular Places

Long Kang Fishing

Join us over @ Longkang Fishing where you can bring your kids here to catch little fishies for only $12 (inclusive of gst), fish tank and fishing net (returnable) will be provided too. Wait no more and come on down to allow your child to relive the olden days hobby of Longkang fishing!

Fishing Paradise @ ORTO

Tired of catching small ass fishes at reservoirs? Say no more, come on over to ORTO's Fishing Paradise where the big bois are at! There are 2 ponds available, a beginner pond and main pond, so even if you are a complete noob you still can have your best fishing experience over here!

Uber Sports Futsal

Uber Sports Futsal here @ ORTO provides the BEST futsal experience you could ever get! Futsal courts are unsheltered, giving you your daily dose of Vitamin D from the sun, so definitely not weakling friendly, HOWEVER, weaklings can still enjoy the futsal experience, because why? WE OPEN 24 HOURS! So MAI TU LIAO and make a reservation @ enquiries@ubersports.com.sg for the best futsal experience you ever gonna get!

Red Dynasty Paintball

Red Dynasty Paintball @ ORTO offers a fun-filled team-building activity for participants of all genders and fitness levels! It is fun, healthy and safe to play! It is the perfect stress reliever whether you are dealing with school or work or whatever you are stressed with! Enjoy shooting your friends and family or even that one colleague you hate at work (YEAH GIVE HIM/HER A TASTE OF HELL)! Participants must be 14 years old and above, parental consent must be produced for participants above the age of 14 and under the age of 18

Maximum Drift Karting Arena

Why settle for standard Go-Karts when you can drift like you are in Initial D over here @ Maximum Drift Karting Arena? Maximum Drift Karting Arena @ ORTO is Singapore's FIRST and ONLY Drift-Karting Arena! While traditional Go-Karts are built with a focus on high speed and straight tracks, it is unable to include a drifting element without any compromise on one’s safety. Therefore, with the introduction of Drift Karts, which does not solely focus on speed but challenges one’s skill at every corner of this thrilling ride with safety ensured. So, forget about just speeding on a straight track and drift like Jay Chou in Initial D SKRRRRT SKRRRRT!
